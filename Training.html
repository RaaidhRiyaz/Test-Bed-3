World-Class Training Facilities

SLSEA operates some of the most advanced cosmonaut training facilities in the world, located at our Batticaloa Spaceport complex.

Neutral Buoyancy Laboratory

12-meter deep pool with full-scale space station modules for EVA training. Simulates microgravity conditions for 6-hour training sessions.

TU22M-S1 Parabolic Flight

Modified supersonic bomber providing 25-second periods of microgravity for adaptation training and short-duration experiment practice.

High-Altitude Survival Center

Simulated mountain and wilderness environments for survival training, including water landings, extreme weather, and emergency procedures.

Centrifuge Complex

20-meter arm centrifuge capable of simulating launch and re-entry G-forces up to 12G with variable acceleration profiles.

Virtual Reality Mission Simulator

Full-immersion VR systems for lunar surface operations, Mars exploration, and complex spacecraft docking procedures.

Space Medicine & Research Center

Advanced medical facility for physiological monitoring, countermeasure development, and long-duration space health research.
