World's Most Advanced

Mission Control Center

SLSEA's Mission Control is a technological marvel featuring redundant systems, AI-assisted operations, and global network connectivity for 24/7 mission oversight.

AI-Assisted Operations

Neural network systems provide real-time anomaly detection and decision support for mission controllers.

Redundant Systems

Triple-redundant communication, power, and computing systems ensure uninterrupted mission control.

Global Network

Connected to 12 international tracking stations and partner agency control centers worldwide.

VR Mission Visualization

Immersive virtual reality systems for 3D mission visualization and anomaly investigation.

Control Room Specifications

Main Control Room

48 console positions with 8K displays, redundant workstations, and AI-assisted decision support systems.

Communications Hub

Secure encrypted links to spacecraft, tracking stations, and international partners with 99.999% uptime.

Security & Redundancy

Underground backup control center, EMP-protected systems, and biometric access control throughout.

Support Facilities

Comprehensive infrastructure supporting every aspect of spaceport operations, from vehicle assembly to crew accommodations.

Vehicle Assembly Building

160-meter high bay for simultaneous assembly of 3 heavy-lift vehicles with clean room conditions.

Propellant Storage Complex

40,000-ton cryogenic storage capacity for liquid hydrogen, oxygen, and methane with automated transfer systems.

Satellite Processing Facility

ISO Class 5 clean rooms for satellite integration, testing, and fueling with vibration isolation systems.

Cosmonaut Training Center

Full-scale simulators, medical facilities, and mission-specific training environments for crew preparation.

Space Research Center

Advanced laboratories for materials science, propulsion research, and space technology development.

Emergency Response

Dedicated fire station, medical center, and search & rescue teams with 60-second response time to any pad.
