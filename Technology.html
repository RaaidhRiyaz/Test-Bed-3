TU22M SUPERSONIC BOMBER CONVERSION PROGRAM

SLSEA's historic acquisition and complete remodelling of 10 Russian Tu22M "Backfire" strategic bombers into three specialized space technology platforms.

In 2021, SLSEA completed a landmark deal with Roscosmos and Russian defense authorities to acquire 10 decommissioned Tu22M strategic bombers. Our engineering teams have completely remodelled these supersonic aircraft into three distinct specialized platforms, creating unique capabilities unmatched by any other space agency.

TU22M-S1

TU22M-S1 "GURU"

Astronaut Training & High-G Simulation Platform
Aircraft Assigned 4 Units
Max Speed Mach 2.3
Ceiling 60,000 ft
Modifications Zero-G Parabolic Flight Capability
Training Capacity 8 Trainees + 2 Instructors

Modified with variable-stability flight systems and parabolic flight capability to simulate microgravity and high-G re-entry profiles for astronaut training.

TU22M-L1

TU22M-L1 "AGNI"

Air-Based Launch Platform
Aircraft Assigned 3 Units
Max Payload 12,000 kg
Launch Altitude 50,000 ft
Orbital Insertion LEO up to 500km
Launch Cost Reduction 65% vs Ground Launch

Modified with reinforced airframe and launch rail system for air-dropping small satellites and space plane prototypes, dramatically reducing launch costs.
