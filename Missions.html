Mission Timeline

2024

Phase 1: Technology Demonstration

Joint testing of habitat prototypes, space plane docking systems, and resource extraction technology at the Batticaloa Spaceport.

2025

Phase 2: Uncrewed Lunar Delivery

First Vimana space plane lunar flight delivering habitat modules, power systems, and robotic construction equipment to the lunar south pole.

2026

Phase 3: First Crewed Mission

International crew including SLSEA cosmonauts establish initial lunar base operations with 30-day surface mission.

2027

Phase 4: Permanent Presence

Rotating crew rotations begin, base expansion, and initiation of lunar resource utilization for fuel and construction.
